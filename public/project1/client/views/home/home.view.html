Welcome to Workout Builder

Create your own workout, add exercises, keep track and progess to a healthy body. Two things your money can't buy is TIME and HEALTH.

1) View Workout
2) Create new workout
3) Make Workout public
4) Add public workout to user list
5) Make different workouts active
6) Search Wger API for various exercises based on term ex: bench, chest, press
